# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, welcoming, and practical for developers with different levels of experience.

Contributors are expected to participate with respect, patience, and good faith.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior:

- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Assume honest mistakes before bad intent.
- Respect different development styles and constraints.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the workflow kit.
- Be careful when discussing security, privacy, or production operations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing credentials, tokens, customer data, or private project details
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code of conduct, contact the maintainers privately when possible. If no private channel exists yet, open a brief issue asking for a maintainer contact without posting sensitive details.
